Who We Are

PT JAPENANSI NUSANTARA

Japenansi Nusantara is Indonesia's leading independent claims service provider founded in 1991. Since first operation in 1993 we have expanded our operation and had a team of 30 adjusters with full access to Mestari Adjusters Sdn Bhd network with specific areas of expertise in property, engineering, construction, marine, casualties, agriculture, aviation, financial, energy.

Our Portfolio

AFFILATION

In 2019 Japenansi Nusantara signed an affiliation agreement with Mestari Adjusters Sdn Bhd. Mestari is a provider of loss adjusting and claims related services in Malaysia with head quarter ocated in Kuala Lumpur. With over 280 people and an annual turnover in excess of RM20 million, Mestari is one of the largest loss adjusting companies serving the Malaysian insurance industry.Over the years, Mestari have expanded our operations to provide our clients a wider office network, to serve them better.
